About webX

We are a minimalist web development team committed to building blazing-fast, accessible, and flexible web apps using only HTML and CSS. Our mission is to simplify the web for developers and end users alike.

Founded in 2025, webX is the result of a single idea: great UX doesn't require heavy frameworks.
